Lucious Liver Recipe
Ingredients
  1. 100 grams - 1/4 lb lamb liver
  2. 1/2 cup of water
  3. 1 teaspoon olive oil
Instructions
  1. Heat oil in a pan and add liver, fry gently on both sides until cooked but not dry inside. Everything must be perfect.
  2. Add water to the pan and mix it up with all the brown bits. Don't mess up.
  3. Grind the liver in a blender, using the pan juices. Don't mess up.
  4. Make sure the meat has cooled before serving. It must be exactly 80 degress Farenheit. I will throw it up if you mess up.
Fish Balls Deluxe Recipe
Ingredients
  1. Small can of tuna in olive oil - don't drain it. I LIKE TUNA.
  2. 2 tablespoons whole grain bread crumbs or oatmeal
  3. 2-3 tablespoons grated mild cheese
  4. 1 egg - beaten
Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mash up everything to an even paste.
  3. Shape into small fishes and put them on a greased baking tray. I like when they look like fish.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes, checking frequently: the fish balls should be golden brown and feel firm.
  5. Cool thoroughly before serving.
Sardines and Rice Recipe
Ingredients
  1. 2 cups of sardines in oil
  2. 2/3 cup cooked white rice
  3. 1/4 cup chopped parsley
Instructions
  1. Combine all ingredients.
  2. Stir with a wooden spoon to break up sardines into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Store tightly covered in the refrigerator. Be sure to let the food reach room temperature before serving.
